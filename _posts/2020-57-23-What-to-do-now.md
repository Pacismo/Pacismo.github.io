**Hello, All.**

Now that I have this website, I want to take a moment to reflect on what steps I want to take to secure my future as a software developer, who, at the moment, wants to get into game development.

So far, I have yet to decide what I want to make for a first project. This website/blog is a great start, and I plan on extending it further in the future. However, there are limitations in the GitHub Pages implementation of Jekyll, such as a limited range of extensions that could be used. For now, though, it will suit my needs.

## What I Will Do

I plan on perhaps doing a few Game Jams or taking on contracts, as I want to expand my portfolio as a software developer. I don't know what I will specialize in, but as I am able to use C/C++, I may end up taking on embedded systems or computer graphics. However, as I did say to someone today, "for all I know, Toby could end up the software guy and I could end up the musician!"

From now on, expect more jokes. I tend to like it *light-hearted* around here. (And, no. I won't be using the \s. You are smart enough to tell if something's a joke)
